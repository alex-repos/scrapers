SUBSTITUTE TEACHER SERVICES (STS)

The District has contracted with Substitute Teacher Services (STS) to provide the District's substitute teacher needs. If you are interested in working at the Wissahickon School District as a substitute teacher starting in the Fall, 2014, please join us at one of our four (4) sessions with STS at the Central office location (601 Knight Road, Ambler, PA). The dates and times are:

April 29, 2014 at 3:00 PM and 6:30 PM

May 1, 2014 at 3:00 PM and 6:30 PM

No appointment is necessary to attend these sign-up and information sessions. You will however, need to bring the following items:

-Act 34 PA Police Clearance - Less than 1 year old

-Act 114 FBI Fingerprinting Clearance - Less than 1 year old

-Act 151 Pa Child Abuse Clearance - Less than 1 year old

-Proof of highest level of education

-TB/Tine test - less than 2 years old

-Resume and 3 professional references (required if you select other STS districts)

-A voided personal check or banking information for direct deposit

-A valid email address

-2 forms of identificiation, one must be picture for federal form I-9

-You will be required to comoplete a PA Act 24 form.

We look forward to seeing you on either April 29th or May 1st
